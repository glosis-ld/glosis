Abstract

The Global Soil Information System (GloSIS) ontology enables the representation of soil related data in semantic format. The ontology has been derived from the UML GLOSIS data model v1.0, and it has been created in line with best practices and methodologies, reusing existing standard models and ontologies, including sosa/ssn for the representation of measurements, or skos for the representation of codelists. The ontology models is richly axiomatized, including explictly all possible restrictions and relations.
